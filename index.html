ABOUT

Danielle is an RMT, originally from the Okanagan, but after living abroad for a couple of years, she settled in Victoria where she graduated from the West Coast College of Massage Therapy. She has always had an interest in health, wellness, and the human body, and was drawn to massage therapy after receiving treatments herself and realizing the benefits it brings to both the body and the mind.

ABOUT

While she enjoys treating a variety of issues, she developed a passion for women’s health during her pregnancy. She believes massage is a powerful tool to support the body during the prenatal and postpartum periods. She strives to be continually learning and growing in her practice and aims to cultivate a sense of calm relaxation while helping clients reach their individual therapeutic goals. Aside from massage, Danielle enjoys yoga, travelling, and exploring the world through the eyes of her young daughter.

CLINIC

Danielle is working along other talented healthcare practitioners who share the same passion as her at Focus Health, a women’s health centered clinic in Oak Bay, Victoria. She offers direct billing to most major insurance companies, and there is free parking at the back of the clinic.
